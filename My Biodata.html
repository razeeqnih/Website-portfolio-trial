About me

Hi! I’m Mohamamad Raziqaiman Bin Mohd Yazid, also known as Razeeq or AgrabanArt!


I’m an aspiring artist and a student currently pursuing a degree in Multimedia Technology. I have a passion for drawing and enjoy spending my time on hobbies like gaming and expanding my collection of figures and collectibles.

I specialize in stylized anime art, particularly human characters.


Over the years, I’ve also gained experience in design work and have taken on various commission projects. In my free time, I love sharing my creative journey and content online through my social media channels.
Born on July 28, 2002, I’m a proud Johorean, and I’m currently studying at Universiti Malaysia Sabah, Kampus Antarabangsa Labuan.
Follow me on my social media to join me on my journey of making a mark in the world!
